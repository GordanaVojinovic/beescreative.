Nature deserves more.

Our biopolymer packaging is more than sustainable — it speaks. Made from renewable materials, fully biodegradable and functional, it proves that brands can care without compromise. The fruits of nature deserve nothing less than nature itself.

Innovation meets intention.

We’ve created a solution where biopolymers replace plastic — realized through a 3D-modeled prototype: visually identical, fundamentally different in purpose.

"Waste Transformed Into Design.

Each prototype is 3D-printed with recycled plastic filament, merging advanced production with environmental responsibility.

Ergonomics with purpose.

Our smallest bottle is child-safe — unbreakable, palm-friendly, and spill-resistant. Each cap is tailored with a unique mechanism, designed to meet specific user needs.

Designed for movement.

The 500 ml bottle features a wider neck for easier drinking. Its ergonomic grip allows for a clip attachment — perfect for physical activity. The base is stable and designed to maintain balance and pressure control.

Smartly shaped for full control.

For larger bottles, a narrow neck reduces spillage, while dual tapered zones make the bottle easy to hold and handle — at any volume.

No more tea bags full of microplastics.

Our packaging lets you mix & scoop loose tea as you like, with a secure, pressure-sealed cap. The best part? It’s biodegradable — and reusable many times.

Sustainability isn’t a trend — it’s a responsibility.

Our commitment to sustainability supports every brand that shares our values. Every piece of promotional material can be a step toward a better future — and we’re here to lead that step forward.
